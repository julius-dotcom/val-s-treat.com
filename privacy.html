Privacy Policy

Your trust is important to us. We are committed to protecting your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

When you place an order via WhatsApp or use our contact form, we collect personal information, including your **name, phone number, email address, and physical delivery address**. This information is necessary for processing your order and delivery.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To process and fulfill your food orders.
  • To communicate with you regarding your order status and delivery updates.
  • To improve our menu offerings and customer service.
  • To send occasional marketing emails (if you opt-in).

3. Data Sharing and Security

Val's Treat **does not sell, trade, or rent** your personal identification information to others. We use appropriate data collection, storage, and processing practices and security measures to protect against unauthorized access or disclosure.

4. Contacting Us About Policy

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: **order@valstreat.com**.
